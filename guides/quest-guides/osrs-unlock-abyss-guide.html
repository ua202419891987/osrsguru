OSRS How to Unlock the Abyss Guide

Unlock the fastest runecrafting method in the game with this complete Abyss miniquest walkthrough

Updated: May 2026 7 min read

The Abyss is one of the most important pieces of content for any serious runecrafter in Old School RuneScape. Unlocking it grants access to the fastest runecrafting experience in the game, the ability to craft combination runes at the Ourania Altar, and most importantly, the runecrafting pouches that are essential for any high-level runecrafter. Despite being a miniquest rather than a full quest, many players put off unlocking the Abyss because it involves traveling into the Wilderness and speaking with a dangerous NPC. In this guide, I will show you exactly how to complete the Enter the Abyss miniquest safely, explain why the Abyss matters, and share the strategies I have used to train Runecrafting to 99 using this method.

Why Unlock the Abyss?

Before we dive into the how, let me explain why this miniquest is so important. The Abyss offers several unique benefits:

  • Instant access to all runecrafting altars: The Abyss provides portals to every altar in the game, eliminating the need to travel across the map.
  • Runecrafting pouches: These pouches allow you to carry extra essence per trip, massively increasing your XP and profit per hour.
  • Ourania Altar access: The ZMI altar is one of the best Runecrafting training methods, and the Abyss is the fastest way to reach it.
  • Abyssal runecrafting: Crafting runes through the Abyss gives double the experience of normal runecrafting, making it the meta for training.

If you are planning to train Runecrafting beyond level 50, unlocking the Abyss is not optional. It is mandatory.

Miniquest Requirements

The Enter the Abyss miniquest has the following requirements:

  • Completion of Rune Mysteries
  • Completion of the quest you are currently doing (if applicable)

That is it. There are no skill requirements, no combat requirements, and no item requirements. This miniquest is accessible to virtually every member account.

However, I strongly recommend having the following before starting:

  • Decent food in case you get attacked in the Wilderness
  • A method to teleport out of the Wilderness (glory, ring of wealth, or teleport tabs)
  • 25+ Runecrafting to immediately use the small pouch reward

Starting the Miniquest

The miniquest begins in Edgeville. Head to the bank and then walk north toward the Wilderness ditch. Just south of the ditch, you will find a man named the Wilderness Mage, who is actually a Zamorakian mage in disguise. Talk to him, and he will explain that he can teleport you to the runecrafting altars instantly, but he needs your help first.

He asks you to deliver a message to the runecrafting guild in Varrock. This seems simple, but here is where it gets interesting. When you agree, he teleports you directly to a dangerous location.

The Wilderness Teleport

The Zamorak mage teleports you to a random location in level 4-6 Wilderness, just north of Edgeville. This area is a common PKing hotspot, so you need to be careful.

Here is my strategy for surviving this teleport:

  1. Before talking to the mage, bank everything you do not need. Only bring food and a teleport method.
  2. Turn on player attack options to "Hidden" or "Right-click only" so you do not accidentally skull on a player.
  3. As soon as you are teleported, look around for other players. If you see a PKer, immediately teleport out and try again.
  4. If the coast is clear, run south toward Edgeville. Do not stop to fight anything.
  5. Cross the Wilderness ditch back into safety.

The teleport location is random, but it is always close to Edgeville. The run takes about 15-30 seconds if you are not interrupted. I have done this miniquest on at least ten accounts, and I have never been PKed during the teleport, but it is always a possibility. Better safe than sorry.

Talking to the Wizards

After escaping the Wilderness, head to the Wizards' Tower south of Draynor Village. Speak with Wizard Sedridor on the basement floor. He will tell you about the Abyss and the threat it poses.

Next, you need to speak with the runecrafting guild leaders. Head to the runecrafting guild entrance, which is located in the same building as the body altar, east of the Crafting Guild and south of Falador. Talk to the two guild leaders, and they will explain the situation further.

Finally, return to the Zamorak mage in Edgeville. He will reveal his true identity and offer to teach you how to access the Abyss.

Entering the Abyss

The Zamorak mage will now teleport you to the Abyss itself. This is a dangerous area filled with aggressive monsters, but you only need to survive long enough to complete the miniquest.

The Abyss has several types of monsters:

  • Abyssal walkers: Level 81, use melee
  • Abyssal guardians: Level 59, use magic
  • Abyssal leeches: Level 41, use melee

All of these monsters are aggressive and can hit fairly hard. However, they are not your objective. Your goal is to find the Dark Mage, who is located in the center of the Abyss.

Here is how to reach him safely:

  1. When you arrive in the Abyss, immediately turn on Protect from Melee if you have 43 Prayer.
  2. Run directly toward the center of the area. Do not stop to fight anything.
  3. The Dark Mage is surrounded by a small safe zone where monsters cannot attack you.
  4. Once inside the safe zone, talk to the Dark Mage to complete the miniquest.

If you do not have Protect from Melee, bring food and just tank the damage while running to the center. The monsters hit up to about 8, so a few sharks will keep you alive.

Miniquest Rewards

Upon completing the miniquest, you receive:

  • A small pouch (holds 3 additional essence, requires 25 Runecrafting)
  • Access to the Abyss for runecrafting
  • Ability to speak with the Dark Mage to repair pouches
  • Access to the Zamorak mage's teleport to the Abyss

The small pouch is just the beginning. After unlocking the Abyss, you can obtain larger pouches by killing abyssal monsters or through other methods.

Getting All Runecrafting Pouches

Runecrafting pouches are the real reward of the Abyss. Here is how to get each one:

  • Small pouch: Reward from the Enter the Abyss miniquest. Holds 3 essence. Requires 25 Runecrafting.
  • Medium pouch: Drops from abyssal monsters in the Abyss. Holds 6 essence. Requires 35 Runecrafting.
  • Large pouch: Drops from abyssal monsters in the Abyss. Holds 9 essence. Requires 50 Runecrafting.
  • Giant pouch: Drops from abyssal monsters in the Abyss. Holds 12 essence. Requires 75 Runecrafting.

The drop rate for pouches is approximately 1/42 from any abyssal monster. The best strategy is to kill abyssal leeches, as they are the lowest level and die quickly.

Here is my pouch farming method:

  1. Enter the Abyss through the Zamorak mage in Edgeville.
  2. Bring ranged or magic gear to safespot the leeches.
  3. Find a spot near the center where you can attack leeches without being hit by walkers or guardians.
  4. Kill leeches until you get the pouch you need.
  5. If your pouches degrade, talk to the Dark Mage in the center to repair them for free.

It usually takes 30 minutes to 2 hours to get all pouches, depending on RNG. Do not get discouraged if the giant pouch takes a while. It is worth the grind.

Abyss Runecrafting Strategy

Now that you have the Abyss unlocked and your pouches ready, here is how to actually use the Abyss for runecrafting:

The Setup

  • Fill your inventory and pouches with pure essence
  • Wear your best weight-reducing gear (Graceful is ideal)
  • Bring a one-click teleport for escaping PKers (glory, ring of dueling, or teleport tablet)
  • Optionally bring an Amulet of Glory to teleport to Edgeville for fast returns

The Process

  1. Teleport to Edgeville using your Amulet of Glory.
  2. Talk to the Zamorak mage and enter the Abyss.
  3. In the Abyss, you must pass through an obstacle to reach the inner ring. The obstacle is random each time: rock (Mining), eyes (Thieving), tendrils (Woodcutting), or gap (Agility).
  4. Once through the obstacle, enter the portal corresponding to the rune you want to craft.
  5. Craft your runes at the altar.
  6. Use a teleport method to return to a bank.
  7. Repeat.

The Abyss gives double the normal runecrafting experience, which makes it the fastest training method for most runes. Nature runes through the Abyss are particularly popular for both XP and profit.

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Bringing valuable gear to the Wilderness: When talking to the Zamorak mage, only bring essentials. PKers do camp this area occasionally.
  • Not using Protect from Melee in the Abyss: The abyssal monsters are aggressive and can pile you. Prayer makes the run to the center trivial.
  • Forgetting to repair pouches: Pouches degrade after a certain number of uses. If a pouch degrades, it holds less essence. Talk to the Dark Mage regularly.
  • Not bringing a teleport: Always have an emergency teleport when runecrafting in the Abyss. The Wilderness entrance is in multi-combat, and PKers do show up.
  • Trying to fight abyssal monsters without prayer: Just run to the center. There is no reason to fight unless you are specifically farming pouches.

Time-Saving Tips

  • Get the Graceful outfit before serious Abyss runecrafting. The run energy savings are massive.
  • Use the Abyss for Ourania Altar (ZMI) runs. The ZMI altar gives random runes and excellent XP rates.
  • Complete the medium Wilderness Diary for noted bone drops from the Abyss monsters. This is mainly useful for ironmen.
  • Keep multiple Amulets of Glory in your bank. They have limited charges and you will go through them quickly.
  • Consider using the Abyss for blood and soul runecrafting after 77 Runecrafting. These are some of the most profitable activities in the game.

Final Thoughts

Unlocking the Abyss is a rite of passage for any serious runecrafter in Old School RuneScape. The miniquest itself is short and simple, but the rewards are game-changing. With access to all altars, runecrafting pouches, and the Ourania Altar, your runecrafting journey becomes significantly faster and more profitable. Do not let the Wilderness element scare you. The risk is minimal if you prepare properly, and the benefits are enormous. Get your pouches, put on your Graceful, and start crafting.
