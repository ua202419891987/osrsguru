OSRS How to Complete Lost City Guide

Unlock Zanaris and the legendary Dragon Dagger in this essential members quest

Updated: May 2026 7 min read

Lost City is one of the most iconic quests in Old School RuneScape. Not only does it grant you access to the mysterious fairy city of Zanaris, but it also unlocks the ability to purchase and wield the Dragon Dagger, one of the most popular special attack weapons in the entire game. Whether you are a new member looking to expand your map or a veteran creating a new account, this quest is absolutely essential. In this complete 2026 guide, I will walk you through every step, share some personal tips from my many playthroughs, and help you avoid the common pitfalls that trip up new players.

Quest Requirements and Preparation

The good news is that Lost City has no skill requirements. However, you must be a member, and you will need to defeat a level 101 Tree Spirit without any weapons or armor. This catch catches a lot of players off guard, so preparation is key.

Required Items:

  • A knife (or any sharp weapon to cut trees)
  • Runes for combat spells if you plan to mage the Tree Spirit
  • Plenty of food: at least 20 lobsters or better
  • One free inventory space for the Dramen Branch

Recommended:

  • 37+ Prayer for Protect from Melee
  • 40+ Magic for effective safespotting
  • Amulet of Glory for quick teleport to Draynor
  • Stamina potions to reduce running time

Starting the Quest

Begin by heading to the Lumbridge Swamp. Just west of the shed that leads to Zanaris, you will find a camp of four adventurers: a warrior, an archer, a wizard, and a monk. Talk to the warrior, who will tell you about a lost city and a leprechaun who knows its secrets. He will mention that they are searching for the city but have had no luck.

Here is the important part: the warrior will refuse to tell you more unless you have started the conversation correctly. Make sure to ask about the lost city and then select the dialogue option that suggests they might not be telling the whole truth. This triggers the next part of the quest.

Finding the Leprechaun

After speaking with the adventurers, head into the Lumbridge Swamp and look for a tree with a "Chop" option that is slightly different from the others. It is located near the northwest edge of the swamp, close to the coastline. If you are having trouble finding it, look for the tree just south of the cave entrance to the Tears of Guthix minigame.

Chop the tree, and a leprechaun named Shamus will appear. Talk to him, and he will tell you that the lost city is indeed real and can be accessed through a shed in the swamp. However, you will need a Dramen Staff to enter. He directs you to the island of Entrana to obtain a branch from the Dramen Tree.

Do not skip this dialogue. Some players rush through and miss the hint about the Dramen Staff, which causes confusion later.

Traveling to Entrana

To reach Entrana, you must take the boat from Port Sarim. The monks who operate the ferry have strict rules: no weapons, no armor, and no combat gear of any kind. This restriction is what makes the upcoming Tree Spirit fight challenging.

Bank everything that could be considered a weapon or armor. You are allowed to bring:

  • Runes for magic
  • Food
  • Potions
  • A knife (needed to cut the Dramen Branch)
  • Jewelry like rings and amulets

If you try to board with prohibited items, the monk will stop you. Do not try to sneak anything past them. It does not work.

The Dramen Tree Cave

Once on Entrana, run to the northwest part of the island where you will find a dungeon entrance. Climb down, and you will enter a cave filled with level 25 zombies and level 31 skeletons. These are not particularly dangerous, but they can chip away at your health if you are not careful.

Navigate through the cave to the southern end, where you will find the Dramen Tree. Attempt to cut a branch from the tree, and a level 101 Tree Spirit will emerge and attack you. This is the boss fight of the quest.

Defeating the Tree Spirit

The Tree Spirit is the reason many players struggle with Lost City. Because you cannot bring weapons or armor to Entrana, you need to get creative. Here are the three main strategies:

Strategy 1: Magic Safespot (Recommended)

This is by far the easiest method. Bring your best combat runes (Fire Bolt or higher) and find the safespot behind the rocks near the Dramen Tree. The Tree Spirit cannot reach you if you stand behind the southern rocks, allowing you to cast spells freely.

From my experience, about 100 Fire Bolts will do the job. Bring extra runes just in case. This method requires minimal food and almost no risk.

Strategy 2: Melee with Fists

If your Attack and Strength are high enough (60+), you can actually punch the Tree Spirit to death. This sounds ridiculous, but it works. Bring plenty of food and use the Protect from Melee prayer if you have it. Expect to eat frequently, as the Tree Spirit can hit up to 10.

Strategy 3: Recoil Ring

A Ring of Recoil can help speed up any of the above methods by reflecting damage back at the Tree Spirit. It is not required, but every bit of damage helps.

Creating the Dramen Staff

Once the Tree Spirit is defeated, use your knife on the Dramen Tree to cut a branch. You may need multiple attempts if your Woodcutting level is low. I recommend having at least level 36 Woodcutting to avoid frustration, though it is possible at lower levels.

With the branch in your inventory, use a knife on it to craft a Dramen Staff. You must have level 31 Crafting to do this. If your Crafting is too low, you will need to train it first or ask a friend to craft it for you on Entrana.

Entering Zanaris

With your newly crafted Dramen Staff, return to Lumbridge Swamp and find the small shed in the center of the swamp. Equip the Dramen Staff and enter the shed. Instead of entering a normal shed interior, you will be transported to Zanaris, the Lost City.

Congratulations, the quest is complete.

Quest Rewards

Upon completion, you will receive:

  • 3 Quest Points
  • Access to Zanaris
  • Ability to purchase and wield the Dragon Dagger
  • Ability to craft Cosmic Runes (if you have the Runecrafting level)

The Dragon Dagger is sold by the salesman in Zanaris for 30,000 gold. Its special attack, Puncture, hits twice with increased accuracy and 15% boosted damage. This makes it a staple weapon for questing, PvM, and even PvP.

Common Mistakes and How to Avoid Them

  • Bringing gear to Entrana: Double-check your inventory before boarding the boat. Even a bronze scimitar will get you turned away.
  • Forgetting the knife: You need a knife to cut the Dramen Branch. Without it, you will have to leave Entrana and return, which is a huge waste of time.
  • Not using the safespot: The Tree Spirit safespot is well-documented and makes the fight trivial. There is no reason to tank it unless you are specifically looking for a challenge.
  • Low Crafting level: Many players forget about the 31 Crafting requirement. Check your level before starting the quest.
  • Not equipping the Dramen Staff: You must have the staff equipped to enter Zanaris. Simply having it in your inventory is not enough.

Time-Saving Tips

  • Use an Amulet of Glory to teleport to Draynor, then run south to the swamp. It is the fastest route.
  • Bring a Dramen Staff on future visits to Zanaris. You will need it every time.
  • After completing the quest, unlock the fairy rings by starting the Fairytale Part II quest. Fairy rings provide the fastest travel in the game.
  • The Zanaris marketplace has a convenient bank and a cooking range nearby, making it a decent mid-level training spot.

Final Thoughts

Lost City is a relatively short quest, but it unlocks some of the most important content in the members' world. The Dragon Dagger alone is worth the effort, and Zanaris serves as a hub for numerous other quests and activities. Follow this guide, prepare properly for the Tree Spirit, and you will have it done in under an hour. Happy questing.
