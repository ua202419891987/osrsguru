OSRS Desert Treasure Quest Guide for Low Level

Complete the master quest that unlocks Ancient Magicks, even with minimal combat stats

Updated: May 2026 10 min read

Desert Treasure is one of the most important quests in Old School RuneScape. It unlocks the Ancient Magicks spellbook, which contains some of the most powerful spells in the game, including Ice Barrage and Blood Barrage. For low-level players, this quest can seem intimidating. It is a master-level quest with four challenging boss fights and a long list of requirements. But here is the truth: Desert Treasure is entirely doable at low combat levels if you know the right strategies. I completed it on a level 50 combat pure, and I am going to show you exactly how I did it.

Quest Requirements

Before you start, make sure you have completed the following quests:

  • The Dig Site
  • Druidic Ritual
  • The Tourist Trap
  • Temple of Ikov
  • Priest in Peril
  • Waterfall Quest
  • Troll Stronghold

Skill Requirements:

  • 10 Slayer
  • 50 Magic
  • 50 Firemaking
  • 53 Thieving
  • 10 Fletching
  • 50 Runecraft
  • 50 Mining

The 50 Magic requirement is the most important. You need at least 50 Magic to cast the spells required during the quest. If you are not there yet, do some splashing or low-level alching to get it up.

Required Items

Bring the following items before starting:

  • 650 coins for magic carpet rides
  • Ashes
  • Blood runes (at least 100)
  • Death runes (at least 100)
  • Charcoal
  • 6 Molten glass
  • 12 Magic logs
  • 6 Steel bars
  • Garlic
  • Silver bar
  • Spice
  • Face mask or Slayer helmet
  • Shantay pass and plenty of waterskins

Starting the Quest

Begin by talking to the Archaeologist at the Bedabin Camp, south of the Shantay Pass. He will tell you about a mysterious diamond and direct you to the Bandit Camp. From here, the quest branches into four paths, each leading to a different diamond guarded by a powerful boss.

You can do the four diamonds in any order. I recommend doing them in this order: Smoke Diamond, Shadow Diamond, Blood Diamond, Ice Diamond. This order minimizes backtracking and puts the hardest fight last, when you are most confident.

The Smoke Diamond - Fareed

Fareed is a level 167 boss who uses melee and fire magic. He is weak to water spells, so bring your best water runes.

Preparation

  • Equip ice gloves (obtained from the Ice Queen in the White Wolf Mountain)
  • Bring plenty of food: sharks or better
  • Water spells: Water Bolt or higher
  • Protect from Melee prayer

The ice gloves are absolutely essential. Without them, Fareed will unequip your weapon every time he hits you, making the fight nearly impossible. Do not attempt this fight without ice gloves.

Strategy

Fareed can be safespotted behind the rocks in his room. Stand behind the southern rocks and cast water spells at him. He cannot reach you, and the fight becomes trivial. Bring about 100 water runes to be safe.

From my level 50 pure run, this fight took about 5 minutes with Water Bolt. Just keep your prayer up and do not get greedy with attacks.

The Shadow Diamond - Damis

Damis has two forms: a level 103 first form and a level 174 second form. The second form drains your prayer rapidly, which makes this fight tricky for low-level players.

Preparation

  • Bring at least 2-3 prayer potions
  • Food: lobsters or better
  • Magic gear with high magic attack bonus
  • Protect from Melee

Strategy

Damis can be safespotted in the Shadow Dungeon. There is a spot near the eastern wall where he gets stuck behind obstacles. Use this to your advantage.

The key to this fight is conserving prayer. Turn on Protect from Melee only when Damis is close enough to attack. When he is stuck behind obstacles, turn it off to save prayer points. This technique is called prayer flicking, and it is essential for low-level accounts.

Alternatively, if your Magic level is high enough, you can simply out-DPS his first form before prayer becomes an issue. The second form hits harder but has less defense.

The Blood Diamond - Dessous

Dessous is a level 139 vampire who uses melee and magic attacks. He is weak to air spells and can be defeated with relatively low stats if you use the right approach.

Preparation

  • Garlic (required to weaken him)
  • Air spells
  • Protect from Magic prayer
  • Decent food supply

Strategy

Before the fight, use garlic on Dessous to weaken his attacks. This is a quest mechanic that many players miss. Without garlic, he hits significantly harder.

Dessous can be safespotted behind the fence in his arena. Stand on the other side of the fence and cast air spells. He cannot pass through the fence, making this one of the easiest fights in the quest.

I have done this fight on multiple accounts, and the safespot makes it a guaranteed win every time. Just bring enough runes and food, and you are fine.

The Ice Diamond - Kamil

Kamil is widely considered the hardest of the four bosses. He is level 154, uses ice magic that freezes you in place, and is surrounded by level 91 trolls that can ruin your day.

Preparation

  • Fire spells: Fire Bolt or higher
  • Plenty of food: sharks highly recommended
  • Super restore potions (his ice attacks drain stats)
  • Protect from Magic prayer
  • Warm clothing to reduce ice damage

Strategy

Kamil can be safespotted, but it is trickier than the other bosses. The most reliable method is to lure him to the western side of his platform and trap him behind the ice formations.

Here is my personal strategy that has never failed:

  1. Run past the trolls without fighting them. They are not worth your time.
  2. Lure Kamil to the western edge of his platform.
  3. Run behind the ice pillar and trap him.
  4. Cast fire spells while keeping Protect from Magic active.
  5. Eat food whenever your health drops below 30.

The trolls are the real danger here. If they catch up to you while you are fighting Kamil, you are in trouble. Move quickly and do not stop to fight them.

The Final Fight - Dessous and the Pyramid

After collecting all four diamonds, return to the Archaeologist and then head to the pyramid southeast of the Bandit Camp. Place the diamonds on the pyramid pedestals to enter the final chamber.

The final fight is against a level 124 enemy who uses all three combat styles. However, by this point, you have already done the hard work. The final boss can be safespotted behind the altar in the center of the room. Use Protect from Magic and cast your best spells.

Once defeated, talk to the mysterious figure to unlock the Ancient Magicks spellbook.

Quest Rewards

  • 3 Quest Points
  • 20,000 Magic XP
  • Access to the Ancient Magicks spellbook
  • Ability to use Ancient teleport tablets

The 20,000 Magic XP is a massive boost for low-level accounts. It can take you from 50 to nearly 55 Magic instantly, which unlocks even more powerful spells.

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Not bringing ice gloves for Fareed: This is the most common mistake. Without ice gloves, the fight is nearly impossible.
  • Forgetting garlic for Dessous: Garlic significantly reduces his damage. Do not skip it.
  • Fighting the trolls before Kamil: The trolls respawn quickly and waste your supplies. Just run past them.
  • Not using safespots: Every boss in this quest can be safespotted. There is no reason to tank them.
  • Underestimating waterskin usage: The desert heat drains your health if you run out of water. Bring extra waterskins.

Time-Saving Tips

  • Use magic carpet rides between locations. They cost a small fee but save massive amounts of time.
  • Set up your inventory before each fight. Organize your runes, food, and potions for quick access.
  • Bring a ring of dueling to teleport to the Duel Arena, which is close to the desert.
  • After completing the quest, buy Ancient teleport tablets from the Grand Exchange for quick travel.

Final Thoughts

Desert Treasure is a challenging quest, but it is absolutely worth the effort. Ancient Magicks opens up entirely new playstyles, from Ice Barrage bursting in the Abyss to Blood Barrage healing at God Wars Dungeon. As a low-level player, do not let the master quest label intimidate you. With the safespots and strategies in this guide, you can complete it at combat level 50 or even lower. Take your time, prepare properly, and enjoy unlocking one of the most powerful spellbooks in the game.
