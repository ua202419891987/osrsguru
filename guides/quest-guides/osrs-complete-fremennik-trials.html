OSRS How to Complete Fremennik Trials Guide

Master all seven trials and become an honorary Fremennik with this complete quest walkthrough

Updated: May 2026 11 min read

The Fremennik Trials is a unique quest in Old School RuneScape that tests your skills across multiple disciplines rather than focusing on a single storyline. To become an honorary member of the Fremennik tribe, you must complete seven different trials, each designed by a prominent Fremennik citizen. These trials range from combat challenges to riddles to crafting tasks, and many players get stuck on one or two of them. I have completed this quest more times than I can count, and I have developed streamlined strategies for each trial. In this guide, I will walk you through every trial with clear instructions, item lists, and the shortcuts that will save you from running back and forth across Rellekka.

Quest Requirements

The Fremennik Trials has no strict skill requirements, which makes it accessible to most member accounts. However, some trials are much easier with higher levels.

Recommended Levels:

  • 40+ Combat stats for the combat trials
  • 40+ Crafting for the crafting trial
  • 40+ Woodcutting for the woodcutting trial
  • 25+ Fletching (helpful but not required)

Required Items (bring all at once to save time):

  • About 5,000 coins
  • A beer (can be bought in Rellekka)
  • Raw shark, raw manta ray, or raw sea turtle
  • Tinderbox
  • Knife
  • Thread
  • Needle
  • Any pickaxe
  • Any axe
  • Pet rock (obtained during the quest)
  • Plenty of food for combat

Starting the Quest

Begin by talking to Brundt the Chieftain in the longhall in Rellekka, the main Fremennik settlement. He will explain that to become a Fremennik, you must gain the support of seven of the twelve council members. Each council member has their own trial for you to complete.

You can do the trials in any order, but I recommend the order below to minimize backtracking and inventory management.

Trial 1: Manni the Reveller's Drinking Contest

This is the easiest trial. Manni the Reveller is in the longhall and challenges you to a drinking contest. Here is how to win:

  1. Talk to Manni and accept the challenge.
  2. He will give you a special Fremennik beer. Drinking it normally will knock you out.
  3. Before drinking, use your normal beer on the Fremennik beer to dilute it.
  4. Now drink the diluted beer. You will stay conscious and win the contest.

If you do not have a normal beer, buy one from the longhall bartender for a few coins. This trial takes about 30 seconds.

Trial 2: Olaf the Bard's Performance

Olaf wants you to perform on stage, but first you need an instrument. He asks you to craft a lyre.

  1. Go to the forest east of Rellekka and find the swaying tree. It is the only tree that moves.
  2. Chop the swaying tree to get a branch.
  3. Use your knife on the branch to get an unstrung lyre.
  4. You need to string the lyre with golden wool. Go to the golden sheep pen southeast of Rellekka.
  5. The golden sheep are behind a fence and cannot be sheared directly. You need to get inside.
  6. Talk to Lalli the troll in the cave nearby. He is guarding the entrance.
  7. Lalli wants a stone soup. Use your pet rock on the stew pot next to him, then talk to him again. He will think you made soup and let you pass.
  8. Shear a golden sheep and spin the wool on a spinning wheel (the one in Rellekka works).
  9. Use the golden wool on the unstrung lyre.
  10. Return to Olaf and play the lyre on stage.

Pro tip: The pet rock is obtained by talking to Askeladden, who is outside the longhall. Do this before talking to Lalli.

Trial 3: Sigmund the Merchant's Favor

Sigmund wants a rare flower called the Exotic Flower in exchange for his vote. This trial involves a trading chain.

  1. Talk to Sigmund. He wants the flower.
  2. Go to the clothing store in Rellekka and talk to Yrsa. She wants a pair of custom boots.
  3. Go to the weapon shop and talk to Skulgrimen. He wants a rare fish.
  4. Go to the docks and talk to the fisherman. He wants a weather forecast.
  5. Go to the helmet shop and talk to Peer the Seer. He will give you a weather forecast if you solve his riddle.

Peer the Seer's Riddle

Peer asks you a riddle about counting items. The answer depends on your account, but the general solution is:

  • Count the number of trees around his house
  • Multiply by the number of ships at the dock
  • Add the number of stalls in the marketplace
  • Subtract the number of people in the longhall

If you get stuck, the answer is usually a number between 10 and 50. Just try numbers systematically if you cannot figure it out.

After getting the forecast, trade it back through the chain:

  1. Fisherman gives you the rare fish
  2. Skulgrimen gives you the custom boots
  3. Yrsa gives you the exotic flower
  4. Sigmund accepts the flower and gives you his vote

Trial 4: Swensen the Navigator's Maze

Swensen has a maze in his basement. You must navigate it to earn his vote.

  1. Enter Swensen's house in the southwest part of Rellekka.
  2. Go down to the basement.
  3. You will see a grid of rooms connected by portals. Each room has a directional name.
  4. The correct path through the maze is:

Solution: South, West, South, East, North, West, South

Follow this exact path, and you will reach the center of the maze where Swensen is waiting. Talk to him to complete the trial.

If you take a wrong turn, you will be teleported back to the start. Just follow the sequence above and you will be fine.

Trial 5: Thorvald the Warrior's Combat Trial

Thorvald wants you to defeat a Koschei the Deathless in combat. This is a multi-phase fight in the basement of the longhall.

The Fight

Koschei has four forms, each stronger than the last:

  • Form 1: Level 91, easy to defeat with any combat style.
  • Form 2: Level 96, slightly harder but still manageable.
  • Form 3: Level 105, hits harder. Use food as needed.
  • Form 4: Level 110, this form has 1 hitpoint but regenerates unless you deal enough damage quickly.

Strategy

For the first three forms, simply attack with your best weapon and eat food when your health drops below 30. Protect from Melee helps significantly.

The fourth form is the tricky one. Koschei regenerates health faster than you can damage him unless you use a fast weapon. Here is the trick:

  • Use your fastest weapon: a dagger or scimitar on rapid/accurate
  • Use a Ring of Recoil to reflect damage
  • Alternatively, use magic with a fast spell like Wind Bolt

The key is consistent, rapid damage. Do not use slow weapons like battleaxes or two-handed swords for the final form.

Important: You do not actually need to kill the fourth form. If you survive for a few minutes without killing him, Thorvald will declare the trial complete anyway. This is a useful backup if you are struggling.

Trial 6: Peer the Seer's Item Retrieval

Peer the Seer wants you to retrieve items from his house without using your hands. This is a puzzle trial.

  1. Enter Peer the Seer's house in the northeast part of Rellekka.
  2. He will ask you to retrieve several items from his house.
  3. The house has various containers and objects. You need to find the correct items.
  4. Key items to find:
  • Unstamped letter: In the chest upstairs
  • Red herring: In the cupboard downstairs
  • Wooden disk: In the chest upstairs
  • Old red disk: In the cupboard downstairs

Use the items on each other in the correct order to create the item Peer wants. The exact combination varies, but generally:

  1. Use the red herring on the range to cook it
  2. Use the cooked herring on the wooden disk
  3. Use the result on the old red disk

If you get stuck, examine each item for clues. Peer will also give hints if you talk to him.

Trial 7: Sigli the Huntsman's Hunt

Sigli wants you to hunt a Draugen, a spirit that appears randomly around Rellekka.

  1. Talk to Sigli to get the Draugen's location.
  2. The Draugen appears at random locations around the Fremennik Province.
  3. Use the talisman Sigli gives you to locate the Draugen. The talisman will glow brighter as you get closer.
  4. When you find the Draugen, attack and defeat it. It is level 96 but not particularly dangerous.
  5. Pick up the Draugen's essence and return it to Sigli.

The Draugen can appear in several locations, including:

  • Near the golden sheep pen
  • Near the swaying tree
  • Near the bridge south of Rellekka
  • Near the lighthouse path

Just run around with the talisman until it glows brightly, then search the area. The Draugen will appear.

Completing the Quest

Once you have the support of seven council members, return to Brundt the Chieftain in the longhall. He will declare you an honorary Fremennik and complete the quest.

Quest Rewards

  • 3 Quest Points
  • 2,812 Attack XP
  • 2,812 Strength XP
  • 2,812 Defense XP
  • 2,812 Hitpoints XP
  • 2,812 Fishing XP
  • 2,812 Thieving XP
  • 2,812 Woodcutting XP
  • 2,812 Crafting XP
  • Access to the Fremennik Province
  • Ability to wear Fremennik armor (helmets)
  • Ability to use the Peer the Seer bank deposit box

The ability to wear Fremennik helmets is a significant reward. The Berserker Helm, in particular, is one of the best melee helmets in the game and requires this quest to equip.

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Not bringing all items at once: The trials require a lot of running around. Bring everything on the item list before starting to save time.
  • Forgetting the pet rock: You need the pet rock for Lalli's soup. Get it from Askeladden early.
  • Using a slow weapon on Koschei's final form: Use a fast weapon or Ring of Recoil. Slow weapons make the final form nearly impossible.
  • Not diluting the beer: Drinking the Fremennik beer straight will knock you out. Always dilute it first.
  • Getting lost in Swensen's maze: Write down the correct path or follow the sequence exactly. Wrong turns reset your progress.

Time-Saving Tips

  • Do Manni's trial first since you are already in the longhall.
  • Do Olaf's trial second since the swaying tree and golden sheep are near each other.
  • Buy all needed items from Rellekka shops before starting. The town has everything you need.
  • For Koschei, bring a super combat potion and your best gear. The fight is not hard with proper stats.
  • After completing the quest, buy a Fremennik helm from the helmet shop. The Berserker Helm is endgame-tier melee gear.

Final Thoughts

The Fremennik Trials is one of the most rewarding early-to-mid game quests in Old School RuneScape. The total experience rewards are substantial, the Berserker Helm unlock is essential for melee training, and the quest itself is a fun change of pace from standard fetch-and-kill quests. Follow this guide, do the trials in a logical order, and you will have this quest completed in under two hours. Welcome to the Fremennik tribe.
