OSRS How to Unlock Fairy Rings Quick Guide

Unlock the fastest travel network in Gielinor with this streamlined Fairytale quest guide

Updated: May 2026 6 min read

Fairy rings are the single most efficient travel method in Old School RuneScape. Once unlocked, they provide instant teleportation to nearly every corner of the map, from the deep Wilderness to the farthest islands. If you are serious about slayer, farming, clue scrolls, or just getting around quickly, unlocking fairy rings should be one of your top priorities. In this quick guide, I will show you the fastest path to activation, what quests you need, and some personal shortcuts I have developed over thousands of hours of gameplay.

Prerequisites: What You Need First

Before you can use fairy rings, you must complete two quests: Lost City and Fairytale Part I - Growing Pains. These are both relatively short members quests, but they are non-negotiable. You cannot skip them.

Lost City unlocks Zanaris and gives you the Dramen Staff. Fairytale Part I introduces you to the fairy godfather and sets up the story for Part II, which is where the magic happens. If you have not done these yet, check out our Lost City guide first.

Fairytale Part II Requirements:

  • Completion of Fairytale Part I and Lost City
  • 40 Thieving
  • 49 Farming
  • 57 Herblore
  • Ability to defeat a level 111 Tanglefoot (safespottable)

The skill requirements are the biggest gatekeeper here. If your Farming or Herblore is not there yet, focus on training those first. I recommend doing herb runs and quests that give Farming XP to get to 49 quickly.

Quick Fairytale Part II Walkthrough

Once you meet the requirements, head to Zanaris and talk to the Fairy Godfather in the large building north of the bank. He will tell you that the fairy queen has fallen ill and needs your help. This begins the quest.

Step 1: Gather the Ingredients

You need to create a magic secateurs to defeat the Tanglefoot. The Godfather sends you to talk to various NPCs to gather components. Here is the streamlined route:

  1. Talk to the Fairy Chef in Zanaris. She needs a special potion.
  2. Go to the bank and grab a vial of water, clean irit, and an eye of newt.
  3. Mix the potion with 57 Herblore to create a Magic Essence potion.
  4. Talk to the Nature Spirit in the Mort Myre Swamp. You need to have completed Nature Spirit quest for this.
  5. The Nature Spirit enchants your secateurs into Magic Secateurs.

Pro tip: Bring a Druid Pouch if you have not done Nature Spirit yet. The swamp can rot your food without it.

Step 2: Defeat the Tanglefoot

The Tanglefoot is located in the Enchanted Valley, accessible through a fairy ring during the quest. Do not worry about the code. The quest automatically transports you there.

The Tanglefoot is level 111 and looks intimidating, but it is actually one of the easiest quest bosses if you use the safespot. Stand behind the rocks in the southeastern part of the valley. The Tanglefoot cannot reach you, and you can safely attack with ranged or magic.

Important: You must use the Magic Secateurs to deal damage. No other weapon works. If you forgot them, you will need to go back and get them.

From my experience, the fight takes about 2-3 minutes with the safespot. Bring some food just in case, but you probably will not need it.

Step 3: Cure the Queen

After defeating the Tanglefoot, pick up the Queen's secateurs from the ground. Return to Zanaris and talk to the Fairy Godfather. He will instruct you to use the secateurs on the queen, who is located in a room accessed from the building.

Once the queen is cured, the quest completes. But here is the critical part: fairy rings are not automatically unlocked just by completing the quest. You need to talk to the Fairy Fixit near the fairy ring in Zanaris after the quest is done. Many players miss this step and wonder why they cannot use the rings.

How to Actually Unlock Fairy Rings

After completing Fairytale Part II, equip your Dramen Staff or Lunar Staff and use the fairy ring in Zanaris. The first time you do this, the Fairy Fixit will appear and explain the system. From that point on, you can use any fairy ring in the game.

The Dramen Staff is required to use fairy rings unless you have completed the elite Lumbridge and Draynor Diary, which permanently removes the staff requirement. This diary reward is absolutely worth pursuing if you use fairy rings frequently, which you will.

Essential Fairy Ring Codes Every Player Should Know

Here are the most useful fairy ring codes that I use on a daily basis:

  • DKR: Edgeville - Perfect for Wilderness content, Chaos Temple, and rev caves.
  • ALQ: Haunted Woods - Great for werewolf agility and clue scrolls.
  • BIP: Island near Mort Myre - Fast access to Barrows and Mort'ton.
  • BJR: Realm of the Fisher King - Required for some clues and quests.
  • CKR: South of Tai Bwo Wannai - Excellent for slayer tasks in the jungle.
  • CIP: Miscellania - Essential for managing your kingdom.
  • CLS: Feldip Hills - Great for chompy bird hunting and red chinchompas.
  • DJP: Tower of Life - Convenient for ardougne content.
  • DLQ: Nardah - Perfect for desert content and pyramid plunder.
  • BKQ: Enchanted Valley - Where you fought the Tanglefoot.

I recommend writing these down or adding them to your notes. After a few days of use, they will become second nature.

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Not talking to Fairy Fixit: This is the number one reason players think their fairy rings are bugged. Talk to him after the quest.
  • Forgetting the Dramen Staff: Always keep a Dramen or Lunar Staff in your bank. You cannot use rings without one.
  • Not doing the elite diary: The Lumbridge elite diary reward that removes the staff requirement is a game-changer. Make it a long-term goal.
  • Ignoring the quest requirements: Fairytale Part II has real skill requirements. Do not start the quest until you meet them.
  • Meleeing the Tanglefoot: There is no reason to melee this boss. The safespot makes it a free kill.

Time-Saving Tips

  • Quest Cape Shortcut: If you have a Quest Cape, you can teleport directly to the Legends' Guild, which is near a fairy ring.
  • Slayer Ring + Fairy Ring Combo: Use your Slayer Ring to teleport to the Stronghold Slayer Cave, then use the nearby fairy ring to reach your destination.
  • House Portal: If your POH is in Rellekka, you can use the fairy ring just outside your house for easy access.
  • Ardougne Cloak: The Ardougne Cloak teleport puts you near a fairy ring north of the monastery.
  • Spirit Tree to Tree Gnome Village: The village has a fairy ring just outside, making it a great hub.

Final Thoughts

Fairy rings transform how you play Old School RuneScape. What used to be a five-minute run across the map becomes a two-second teleport. The upfront investment in completing Fairytale Part II pays dividends every single time you log in. Get those skill requirements, knock out the quest, talk to the Fairy Fixit, and never look back. Your future self will thank you every time you type a three-letter code and skip across Gielinor.
