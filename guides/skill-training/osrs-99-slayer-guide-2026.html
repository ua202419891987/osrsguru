OSRS Slayer Guide 1-99 2026 — Best Masters, Block List & Tasks

Slayer is the most profitable combat skill in Old School RuneScape, offering access to unique monsters, valuable drops, and essential PVM unlocks. From the abyssal whip at level 85 to the occult necklace at 93, Slayer gates some of the game's most important items. This guide covers optimal master selection, a comprehensive block and skip strategy, task prioritization, and reward unlocks to take you to 99 efficiently.

Best Master at Each Stage

  • Levels 1-30: Turael for fast, simple tasks to reach Vannaka requirements
  • Levels 30-50: Vannaka — decent variety, unlocks important monsters
  • Levels 50-70: Chaeldar, then Nieve at 85 Combat
  • Levels 70-99: Nieve/Steve for consistent XP, Duradel at 100 Combat for highest XP tasks

Konar vs Nieve/Duradel: Konar assigns tasks in specific locations and drops brimstone keys on-task (1 per ~100 kills). Keys average 120K gp each. Use Konar every 10th task for the 50-point bonus, then return to Nieve/Duradel for better XP rates. Konar's location restrictions often prevent cannon and burst setups, reducing XP/hr significantly.

Turael Boosting & Point Farming

Turael assigns the easiest tasks (birds, cows, goblins, etc.) that take 2-5 minutes each. This is used for "boosting" — completing rapid Turael tasks to build up a streak, then switching to your preferred master for every 10th, 50th, 100th, etc. milestone task to claim bonus points.

Point farming strategy:

  1. Complete 9 rapid Turael tasks
  2. For the 10th task, use Konar for maximum points (90 bonus)
  3. Repeat until you have accumulated sufficient points for all desired unlocks
  4. Then switch to Duradel/Nieve for normal training
Warning: Using Turael to skip a task from another master will RESET your task streak to zero. Only use Turael for skipping if you have no points and don't care about your streak. Use Turael for point farming BEFORE building up a streak with Duradel.

Optimal Block & Skip List

With a maximum of 6 block slots (7 with Elite Lumbridge & Draynor Diary), you should block the highest-weighted bad tasks for your chosen master. For Duradel (the best XP master from 100 combat), the optimal block list is:

Task Weight Reason to Block
Hellhounds 10 High HP, no drops except clue scrolls. Terrible XP and gp.
Drakes 8 Slow, annoying special attack, mediocre loot.
Black Demons 8 Slow unless using demonic gorillas (requires MM2).
Spiritual Creatures 8 Low XP, low profit. Skip unless you specifically need them.
Kalphites 9 KQ is slow. Workers have no drops. Block unless cannoning.
Greater Demons 9 No valuable drops. Block unless doing K'ril at GWD.
Suqahs (7th slot) 8 OK with cannon but far from bank — worth blocking.

Tasks to skip (with points) when assigned: Iron dragons, steel dragons, waterfiends, cave kraken (post-trident), wyverns (if not hunting visage), and blue dragons (unless Vorkath).

Tasks to extend (with Slayer reward unlocks): Nechryaels, dust devils, abyssal demons, smoke devils (burst tasks for XP), gargoyles, dark beasts (profit tasks).

Cannon Tasks vs Non-Cannon

The dwarf multicannon dramatically speeds up multi-combat slayer tasks. It costs about 400K gp/hr in cannonballs but doubles or triples XP rates on suitable tasks.

Burst & Barrage Tasks

Burst and barrage tasks are among the fastest Slayer XP in the game. Stack monsters in a 3x3 area and hit up to 9 at once with Ancient Magicks.

Task Level Requirement Location XP Rate Profit
Dust Devils 65 Slayer Catacombs of Kourend ~80K-100K Slayer + 100K Magic XP/hr 500K+ gp/hr
Nechryaels 80 Slayer Catacombs of Kourend ~90K-120K Slayer + 200K Magic XP/hr 800K+ gp/hr
Smoke Devils 93 Slayer Smoke Devil Dungeon ~100K-140K Slayer + 250K Magic XP/hr 1M+ gp/hr
Abyssal Demons 85 Slayer Catacombs of Kourend ~80K-100K Slayer + 150K Magic XP/hr 500K+ gp/hr
Jellies 52 Slayer Catacombs of Kourend ~40K-50K Slayer + 80K Magic XP/hr 100K+ gp/hr
Pro Tip: Use the Catacombs of Kourend for burst/barrage tasks whenever possible. The catacombs restore prayer when burying bones (Arceuus spellbook or bonecrusher), meaning unlimited prayer with bonecrusher necklace. Stack monsters by running to a corner and letting them pile up, then freeze with ice spells.

Superior Slayer Monsters

Superior monsters have a 1/200 chance of appearing when killing a monster on-task. They're larger, stronger versions with unique drop tables including the imbued heart (30M+ gp, magic level boost) and eternal gem (5M+ gp, used for the ring of endurance/suffering). Always kill superiors when they spawn — the XP drop is triple the normal monster and the unique drops are game-changing.

Slayer Helm Progression

  • Black Mask (400 pts): Purchased from Slayer masters. Gives 16.67% melee accuracy and damage on-task. Absolute priority.
  • Slayer Helmet (400 pts): Combine black mask with nose peg, earmuffs, facemask, and spiny helmet. Works as all four protection items. Required for dust devils and smoke devils.
  • Slayer Helmet (i) (1.25M NMZ pts or 500 Slayer pts): Adds 15% ranged and magic damage on-task. Essential.
  • Slayer Helmet (colored variants): Cosmetic only. Requires 1,000 Slayer points.

Ironman Slayer Notes

For ironmen, Slayer is arguably the single most important skill. It provides:

  • Level 85: Abyssal whip (primary melee weapon)
  • Level 87: Trident of the seas (essential magic weapon for Zulrah, raids)
  • Level 91: Cerberus crystals for primordial/eternal/pegasian boots
  • Level 93: Occult necklace (10% magic damage, acquired from smoke devils)
  • Level 95: Hydra leather and claw for ferocious gloves and dragon hunter lance
  • Seeds: Nechryaels and dagannoths drop herb seeds essential for Herblore
  • Alchables: Training burst tasks generates millions of GP for kingdom and supplies
Ironman Tip: Unlock "Bigger and Badder" immediately for superior monsters. The imbued heart is one of the most impactful items for ironmen, providing a repeatable magic level boost for all bosses. Unlock the herb sack (750 pts) and rune pouch (750 pts from Slayer or LMS) before doing extensive burst/barrage tasks.
