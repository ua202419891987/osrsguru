OSRS How to Get to Kourend Castle: Quick Travel Guide for 2026

Kourend Castle is the central hub of Great Kourend, OSRS's massive western continent. It's where you'll find the Catacombs of Kourend entrance (arguably the best slayer dungeon in the game), the Woodcutting Guild, the Shayzien area for lizardman shamans, and more. The problem is, the first time you try to get there, you'll probably open your world map, squint at the giant landmass west of the mainland, and wonder where exactly you're supposed to click. This guide covers every method to reach Kourend Castle quickly, ranked from absolute fastest to situational alternatives, so you can get where you're going without wasting time wandering around Zeah.

Method 1: Xeric's Talisman — The Gold Standard

If you learn one method from this guide, make it this one. The Xeric's Talisman is by far the fastest and most versatile way to reach Kourend Castle and the surrounding areas. It's an untradeable amulet that can be mounted in your POH (in a Jewelry Box once you hit 81 Construction), and it offers teleports to multiple locations across Great Kourend, including one directly outside Kourend Castle.

How to Get a Xeric's Talisman

The talisman drops from lizardmen (the standard ones in the Lizardman Canyon, not the shamans). The drop rate is 1/250 from regular lizardmen. You'll need to kill them on-task or off-task — but since lizardmen are a popular slayer task anyway (for the chance at a Xeric's Talisman, which can later be upgraded to the mounted version), most players naturally acquire one during their Slayer grind. The drop is also available from lizardman shamans at 1/125, which is how most players end up with one.

Once you have the talisman, you charge it with Lizardman Fangs (also dropped by lizardmen and shamans). Each fang provides a certain number of teleport charges. The talisman can hold up to 1,000 charges, and fangs are stackable, so charging it once lasts a very long time.

Using the Talisman to Reach Kourend Castle

Rub the talisman, and from the teleport menu, select "Xeric's Look-out" to land directly south of Kourend Castle, just a few steps from the castle's southern entrance. This is the fastest way to reach the castle from anywhere in Gielinor. The whole process takes about 8 seconds from clicking the talisman to being at the castle doors.

Alternative talisman teleport options include "Xeric's Glade" (puts you at the Hosidius area, useful for Tithe Farm) and "Xeric's Inferno" (Lovakengj area, near the Blast Mine), but "Xeric's Look-out" is the one you want for Kourend Castle access.

Pro Tip for Ironmen: Lizardman fangs are a common drop from lizardman shamans, and you'll kill thousands of them on the way to getting a Dragon Warhammer. By the time you even start the DWH grind, you'll have more fangs than you'll ever use. Don't worry about "conserving" your talisman charges — you'll passively accumulate fangs faster than you spend them.

Method 2: Skills Necklace to Woodcutting Guild

If you don't have a Xeric's Talisman yet, the Skills Necklace is your next best option. This is a tradeable item on the GE costing about 20K each, and it has 4 charges. From the necklace's teleport menu, select "Woodcutting Guild". This places you directly inside the Woodcutting Guild in the southern part of Hosidius.

From the Woodcutting Guild, run north past the sand crabs (watch out, they're aggressive if you're low level), cross the bridge, continue north through Hosidius past the farming patches, and you'll reach the southern gates of Kourend Castle. The run takes about 45 seconds to 1 minute depending on your run energy and whether you have stamina potions. Not as fast as the Xeric's Talisman but very reliable and accessible from a cheap item.

Method 3: Fairy Ring (Free, But Slow)

Fairy ring code DJR places you at the north side of the Kourend swamps, east of the castle. From this fairy ring, run west across the bridge and you'll reach the eastern side of Kourend Castle. The run is about 30-40 seconds from the fairy ring, and the ring is completely free to use once you've completed the Fairy Tale quest series up to Part II (which gives access to fairy rings).

While DJR is the farthest from the castle, it's the best free option for early-game accounts that haven't unlocked the talisman yet. If you're doing a lot of Catacombs of Kourend slayer tasks, use the DJR fairy ring, run to the castle, and enter the catacombs from the statue in the castle courtyard. It's a well-worn path that most players use at some point.

Method 4: POH Portal (Endgame Convenience)

If you have 50+ Construction, you can build a Kourend Castle portal in your house's portal chamber. The portal requires 100x Law Runes, 200x Blood Runes, and 200x Soul Runes (expensive, but a one-time cost for unlimited teles). Once built, you can teleport from your POH portal chamber directly to the courtyard of Kourend Castle. Combine this with a house teleport tablet, and your total travel time is about 12 seconds — house tab, click portal, you're there.

This is the ultimate convenience method for players who frequently visit Kourend Castle (for slayer tasks, farming contracts at the Farming Guild just south, or Woodcutting Guild activities). The rune cost is about 100K GP for the portal, which pays for itself within days if you're visiting Kourend multiple times per session.

Method 5: Chronicle Teleport + Running (Early Game Only)

For absolute early-game accounts that don't have fairy rings, skills necklaces, or Xeric's Talisman, the Chronicle (reward from the Demon Slayer quest, costs 300 GP from Django in Draynor) can be a workable starter option. Use the Chronicle to teleport to the Champions' Guild, run south of Varrock to the docks, and take the boat to Port Piscarilius (the southern Kourend port). From Piscarilius, run north through the Kourend city streets to reach the castle. Total travel time: about 2 minutes. This is the worst option by a mile, but it exists if you genuinely have no other teleports available.

Don't make my mistake: My first trip to Kourend Castle, I didn't realize the Woodcutting Guild teleport existed. I used a Chronicle to the Champions' Guild, ran to Port Sarim, took a boat to Zeah, and then spent 5 minutes navigating through Piscarilius's winding streets before finding the castle. All of that could have been avoided with a 20K skills necklace. Spend the money on teleport items. Your playing time is worth more than 20K GP.

What's Actually At Kourend Castle?

Now that you know how to get there, here's what makes Kourend Castle worth visiting:

Catacombs of Kourend

The entrance is a statue in the castle courtyard. This is one of the best slayer dungeons in OSRS because monsters inside have boosted stats and can be bursted/barraged for incredible Slayer XP rates. Dust Devils, Nechryaels, Greater Demons, and Abyssal Demons all have spots in the Catacombs. Additionally, every monster in the Catacombs drops Ancient Shards and Dark Totem pieces, which combine to summon Skotizo — a demon boss with a 100% drop rate of a guaranteed 50K-200K GP clue scroll reward.

Farming Guild (Just South)

Located immediately south of Kourend Castle (you can see it from the castle's southern gate), the Farming Guild is the premier Farming hub in OSRS. Farming contracts, Hespori boss fights, herb patches, tree patches, and the anima patch for farming boosts are all inside. Having a fast route to Kourend Castle gives you a fast route to the Farming Guild for your farming runs.

Kourend Favour System Access

From Kourend Castle, you can access all five houses of Great Kourend: Hosidius (south, farming), Shayzien (east, combat), Lovakengj (north, mining/smithing), Piscarilius (west, fishing/thieving), and Arceuus (northwest, magic/runecrafting). If you're working on Kourend Favour — which unlocks everything from the Arceuus spellbook to the Hosidius kitchen for cooking — the castle is your central launching point.
